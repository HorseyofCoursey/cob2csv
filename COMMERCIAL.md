# Commercial Licensing

cob2csv is free for personal and non-commercial use under the
[PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0](LICENSE).

If you or your organization want to use cob2csv in a commercial context,
a commercial license is required.

## What requires a commercial license

- Use within a for-profit organization, even for internal tooling
- Integrating cob2csv into a paid product or service
- Using cob2csv to process data on behalf of paying customers
- Government contracting work where the output is a commercial deliverable

## What does NOT require a commercial license

- Personal projects
- Academic or research use
- Open source projects (with attribution)
- Evaluation and testing before purchasing
